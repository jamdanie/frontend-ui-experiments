# RidgeRelay — Disclaimer

RidgeRelay is an **early-stage concept and wireframe prototype** created for academic, design, and portfolio purposes only.

This project is **not a production safety system** and **must not be relied upon for real-world emergency response, navigation, or rescue coordination**.

---

## No Emergency Services

- RidgeRelay does **not** connect to emergency services (911, park rangers, SAR, EMS).
- RidgeRelay does **not** guarantee monitoring, alerts, or rescue.
- Any references to escalation or responder handoff are **conceptual only**.

---

## No Guarantees of Accuracy or Coverage

- Location, timing, and connectivity concepts shown in this prototype are simulated.
- Offline maps, connectivity zones, and alerts are illustrative and not authoritative.
- Real-world terrain, weather, and signal conditions are unpredictable.

---

## Not a Substitute for Outdoor Safety Practices

RidgeRelay is **not a replacement** for:
- proper trip planning
- navigation skills
- weather awareness
- personal safety equipment
- satellite communication devices
- informing trusted contacts of your plans

Users should always follow established outdoor safety best practices.

---

## Data & Privacy (Prototype Context)

- This wireframe does not collect, store, or transmit real user data.
- Any references to data handling are illustrative and describe **potential future design intent**, not implemented behavior.

---

## Academic & Portfolio Use

This project exists to explore:
- safety-first UX design
- intent-aware systems
- privacy-by-design principles
- low-signal environment constraints

It is intended for **learning, discussion, and evaluation**, not deployment.
